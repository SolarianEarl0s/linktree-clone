Characters

The people the world turns on.

Each carries a burden. Each makes a choice. Not all of them survive it.

J

Jack

"He didn't ask to be the one."

The Reluctant Hero

Jack doesn't want the burden he's been given. He wants to protect the people next to him. That gap — between what he wants and what's required — is where his story lives.

R

Rose O

"She was the warning no one heeded."

The One Who Breaks the Pattern

Rose sees what others miss and feels what others suppress. Her arc doesn't end with survival — it ends with transformation. What she becomes after Act IV changes everything.

U

Unity

"She holds them together. Even when she's falling apart."

The Anchor

Unity is the emotional core of Act I. She doesn't have the answers — she has the will to keep moving when answers aren't enough. That's what makes her essential, and what makes her vulnerable.

Juan Souls

"He already knows how it ends."

The Dancing Death

A twisted entity that sees what others can't: the future. He doesn't guess. He doesn't hope. He knows. And with that knowledge, he moves to break the only outcomes that could stop what's coming.

Alara

"She was there... before and after."

The Seer. The Mentor. The Sister.

A quiet, mysterious presence seen only in fragments in Act I. She appears when the world begins, and again when it nears its end. Her name is not spoken until Act II.

Ar

Arvel

"He lost everything. He kept going."

The One Who Carries the Weight

Arvel doesn't wear his burden loudly. He carries it quietly, and that's what makes him dangerous. His story isn't about power. It's about what someone does when they've already lost everything.
